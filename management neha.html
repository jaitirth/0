Our Team


Board Of Directors:






Dr. Dileep Tiwari
CEO and founder
Dr. Dileep Tiwari, the CEO and founder of our company, has an impressive academic and professional background. He holds a graduate degree in Life Sciences and postgraduate degree in Biotechnology from A.P.S. University, Rewa, M.P., India. He has a doctorate degree in Biotechnology - Infectious Diseases, with his Ph.D. research conducted at the ACBR in the University of Delhi, Delhi, India, and he has done post-doctoral research at UKZN, Durban, South Africa. With over 21 years of experience, Dr. Tiwari has expertise in R&D, bioinformatics, diagnostics, clinical research, bio-therapeutics research, product development, and the manufacturing of diagnostic kits, bio-therapeutic products, and herbal product development and manufacturing. He also has experience in medical IoT-based tools and software development.
Currently, He is an Hon. Associate Professor in the Discipline of Pharmaceutical Sciences at the School of Health Sciences, University of KwaZulu-Natal, Durban, South Africa, and an Adjunct Professor at People's University, Bhopal, India. Additionally, He serves as an advisor to various institutions & industries and he is Co-Founder and Directore of Highlake Technology. He has published more than 50 research papers in national and international reputed journals and has filed more than 5 patents. Dr. Tiwari has held prestigious positions, including NRF Research Scientist in the Department of Health Sciences at the University of KwaZulu-Natal, Durban, South Africa, and Post-doctoral Researcher in the School of Chemistry, College of Agriculture, Engineering & Science, KwaZulu-Natal University, Durban, South Africa. His industry experience includes roles such as Assistant Manager-R&D at Gennova Biopharmaceutical Ltd, Pune, India, and positions at Zydus Research Centre, Ahmedabad; Bhabha Atomic Research Centre, Mumbai, India; Lupin Labs, India; and ACBR, University of Delhi, Delhi, India.




Dr. Anil Chuturgoon
Director
He is an Independent and Non-Executive Director of our Company. Dr. Chuturgoon is Professor and Head of Discipline of Medical Biochemistry in the School of Laboratory Medicine and Medical Sciences at the University of KwaZulu-Natal (UKZN), Durban South Africa. He is also currently the Dean of Research (acting) in the College of Health Sciences.He completed his BSc, BSc (Hons) and MSc degrees at the University of Natal He thereafter obtained his doctoral degree in toxicology and epigenetics from UKZN.
His research interests are in medical sciences, toxicology and applied biotechnology. He is focussed on determining biomarkers for disease using epigenetic markers such as microRNA and long non-coding RNA.He has been the recipient of several research grants. He is passionate about research and shaping young minds to think critically. He is a member several scientific associations (South African Association of Clinical Biochemists and South African Society of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology) and serves as editorial in Nature (Scientific Reports). He was a recipient of the Distinguished Teacher Award for excellence in teaching at the University of Natal. He is an NRF rated scientist and was placed in the top 30 UKZN researchers for 2016, 2017, and 2018. He has successfully supervised over 50 Masters and 27 doctoral students. He has over 115-refereed publications, 12 chapters in books and over 200 conference proceedings.
