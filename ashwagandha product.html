Product Description:
Ashwagandha is a traditional herb used in Ayurvedic medicine to reduce stress and improve overall well-being.

Key Features: Ashwagandha is used for arthritis, anxiety, bipolar disorder, attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD), balance, obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD), trouble sleeping (insomnia), tumors, tuberculosis, asthma, a skin condition marked by white patchiness (leukoderma), bronchitis, backache, fibromyalgia, menstrual problems, hiccups, Parkinson's disease, and chronic liver disease.

Usage: The typical dosage of ashwagandha ranges from 300 to 500 mg of a standardized extract taken once or twice daily. However, it's best to consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
